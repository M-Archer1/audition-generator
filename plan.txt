# Scale Generator
Randomly create a message that requests a musician to play a certain scale on a certain instrument in major or minor.

Player # 1 (etc, each time it is refreshed the player number should increase by 1)

## Random Elements
* instrument
* scale letter
* major or minor

### Instrument Options
- Flute
- Oboe
- Clarinet
- Alto Sax
- French Horn
- Trumpet
- Trombone
- Baritone
- Tuba
- Violin
- Viola
- Cello
- Bass

### Scale Letters
- C, F, B-flat, E-Flat, A-flat, D-flat, G-flat
- G, D, A, E, B
